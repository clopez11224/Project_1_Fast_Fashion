Fast Fashion

A term used to describe the clothing industry business model of replicating trendy clothes in the most fastest and cheapest way possible which causes major pollution.

This is a short video about Fast Fashion

The fast fashion industry produces greenhouse gasses from production, manufacturing and transportation of garments.

The fast fashion industry contributes to water pollution by dumping chemicals and dyes into nearby water sources and using freshwater to produce garments.

The fast fashion industry contributes to land pollution by adding waste to landfills.
